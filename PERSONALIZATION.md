## Daily Posting Tips

**Best practices for consistent blogging:**

1. **Batch writing**: Write 4 posts at once, schedule throughout month
2. **Consistency**: Publish same day/time each week
3. **Variety**: Mix technical, career, and personal topics
4. **Promotion**: Share on LinkedIn, Twitter, HackerNews
5. **Engagement**: Respond to comments and questions
6. **Quality over quantity**: One great post beats three mediocre ones

**Post structure that works:**
- Hook: Start with a problem or insight
- Context: Why this matters
- Solution/Insight: Your main content
- Examples: Real code or case studies
- Takeaway: Clear conclusion and next steps
